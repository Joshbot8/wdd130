Autobots

  • Optimus Prime

    Optimus Prime is the embodiment of compassion, honor and cour- age. He believes that freedom is everyone's right, and he will fight tirelessly to protect his troops and any and all innocent creatures to the extent that he would sacrifice his own life if it meant saving another.

  • Jazz

    Jazz is one of the fastest, most nimble Autobots and is also known for keeping his cool no matter how bad the situation might seem. His known veichle mode is a Cybertronian sports car.

  • Cliffjumper

    Cliffjumper may not be the largest Autobot around, but he doesn't let that get in the way of picking fights with Decepticons. Fearless and brash, Cliffjumjper's favorite weapons happen to be his fists.

  • Bumblebee

    Bumblebee is a small, energetic, and determined Autobot. He's an excellent scout and messenger because of his small size and speed. He can get into places that other bots can't, which makes him good for sabotage.

  • Grimlock

    Grimlock is the leader of the Dinobots and an Autobot. He views Optimus Prime as a weak leader, too afraid to make the tough calls and act on instinct. As such Grimlock is more than ready to disobey orders if he thinks the ends will justify the means. His one obvious redeeming feature is his attachment to his team.

  • Jetfire

    Jetfire is an Autobot scientist, jet, and a Seeker, who believes that technology is the way forward, used to be originally a keeper of Dark Energon. He is a dedicated scientist whose personal upgrades to his chassis have made him the fastest Autobot on Cybertron.

  • Metroplex

    He is a towering Autobot that is covered from head to toe in heavy artillery weaponry. He is the last line of defense for the Autobots and will respond only to the commands of the last Prime. This also allows Optimus to sync with Metroplex's targeting systems and mark targets for Metroplex to destroy.

Decepticons

  • Megatron

    Megatron is the founder of the Decepticon uprising. He is charismatic, violent, and full of rage for anyone who would stand in the way of his ambition. He believes in a grand purpose for his race and wants to win control of Cybertron for his faction and exterminate the Autobots.

  • Starscream

    The air commander of the Seekers, Starscream is as ambitious as they come. During the early days of the war, Starscream sold the services of the Seekers to Megatron in order to one day usurp his Decepticon master and take command of Cybertron for himself.

  • Vortex

    DVortex is a Decepticon, and the Combaticon's interrogator, with no scruples as to how his methods affect the subject. No Autobot secret is safe when Vortex is around. Vortex's vehicle mode is a Cybertronian style helicopter/jet.

  • Onslaught

    Onslaught believes precision and planning are the keys to success, He spends hours devising battle plans considering contingencies, and deliberating troop disposition before ever committing his forces to battle. Once the fight is joined, however, no robot is a fiercer fighter.

  • Swindle

    Swindle is a Decepticon Combaticon from the Fall of Cybertron portion of the Aligned continuity family. Who's got two thumbs and loads of personality in his box art? Unlike his fellow Decepticons, Swindle doesn't really care about the fate of the universe.

  • Blastoff

    Blast Off is a speed demon who is reckless about his speed. He may seem disgusted by those who can't reach the orbit on their own, but this is just a front to hide his loneliness.

  • Brawl

    Armed with a blaster, Brawl can fire on the Autobots or transform into a Cybertronian tank. What's more, he can combine with the rest of the team to form the giant robot Bruticus!

  • Bruticus

    Bruticus was formed by the Combaticons when they were making a last ditch effort to salvage a mission to capture a large Autobot energon transport. After the team disabled the anti-air guns, the got their dropship close enough for all five of them to depart onto the ship and combine.
